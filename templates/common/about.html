About the idea:

Travel manager helps you organize all your travels. Here you can keep track of the places you have visited and your dream destinations. You can also share pictures of interesting places. Every user can "like" and "comment" the shared pictures in the gallery.

In the "Visited places" section you can manage the trips you have already been to. Every user can create, view, edit and delete a trip.

The "Dream destinations" section is for the places you wish to visit in the future. Every user can create, view, edit and delete a destination.

Feeling social? Check out the gallery section where you can share a photo, see other posts and interact with them.
